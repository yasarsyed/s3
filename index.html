One of the key elements of a business website’s design elements, the Disclaimer is a good start to meeting some of your legal obligations associated with owning a website and the information contained within it. A disclaimer should clearly state the limitations of liability when using the website and the information it contains, along with an explanation of the relationship with any external links. Devoting an entire page to a disclaimer is not always necessary, at times disclaimers are found under general ‘Terms and Conditions’ or mixed in with privacy policies or copyright statements.
